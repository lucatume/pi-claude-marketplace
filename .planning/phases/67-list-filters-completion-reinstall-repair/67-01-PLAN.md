<objective>
Retire the reinstall-local `--force` flag and make reinstall an unconditional
repair primitive that always overwrites collisions and foreign content
(RINST-01 / D-67-03).

Purpose: reinstall's command-local `--force` was an overload of the new
install/update `--force` (Phase 65). Removing it eliminates the last overloaded
meaning of `--force` on the command surface and turns reinstall into a pure
repair primitive whose overwrite is always-on. `reinstall --force` now errors as
an UNKNOWN flag — a clear signal the contract changed rather than a silent no-op.

Output: orchestrator force-option plumbing deleted across all 6 sites; overwrite
made unconditional; edge handler, router help, and completion provider stripped
of reinstall `--force`; coupled tests rewritten to the inverted contract; prose
docs cleaned of any stale `reinstall --force` reference (D-67-04 lockstep).
</objective>
