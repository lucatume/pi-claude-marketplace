**File:** `docs/output-catalog.md`

**Section heading:** `## /claude:plugin marketplace autoupdate <enable|disable> <name>` -- byte-identical to before (catalog-UAT runner keys on this heading verbatim).

**Section range:** Lines 838-898 (was 838-882). **New per-state blocks (5):**

| State | Catalog-state marker | Byte form | Severity | Reload-hint |
|-------|----------------------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| `enable-fresh` | `<!-- catalog-state: enable-fresh -->` | `● foo [user] (autoupdate enabled)\n\n/reload to pick up changes` | info (no severity arg) | yes |
| `disable-fresh` | `<!-- catalog-state: disable-fresh -->` | `● foo [user] (autoupdate disabled)\n\n/reload to pick up changes` | info (no severity arg) | yes |
| `enable-idempotent` | `<!-- catalog-state: enable-idempotent -->` | `● foo [user] (skipped) {already enabled}` | `"warning"` | no |
| `disable-idempotent` | `<!-- catalog-state: disable-idempotent -->` | `● foo [user] (skipped) {already disabled}` | `"warning"` | no |
| `failure-not-found` | `<!-- catalog-state: failure-not-found -->` | `⊘ missing-mp [user] (failed)` | `"error"` | no |

Each block has the prescribed sub-heading (`### Fresh enable`, `### Fresh disable`, `### Idempotent enable`, `### Idempotent disable`, `### Failure -- marketplace not found`), the `<!-- catalog-state: ... -->` discriminator, a `text` fence with the byte form verbatim, and a one-line explainer naming the field-level shape (`mp.status`, optional `mp.reasons`), the severity routing, and the reload-hint disposition (per D-17.1-05 ladder rules).

**Intro paragraph:** 1 paragraph stating the marketplace autoupdate surface flips the marketplace persistence record's autoupdate flag with no plugin children, that V2 distinguishes 5 outcomes via the per-state blocks below, and cross-references the list-surface `<autoupdate>` marker on `## /claude:plugin marketplace list` (the marker is reserved for the list surface and is NOT emitted on state-change arms).

**Closing paragraph:** 1 paragraph summarizing the severity ladder (fresh → info, skipped → warning, failed → error) and the reload-hint ladder (fresh triggers, skipped/failed suppress), cross-referencing D-16-11 + D-17.1-05.

**Deleted prose:** the obsolete `### Enable across multiple marketplaces` (`enable-mixed`), `### Disable` (`disable-mixed`), and old `### Failure -- marketplace not found` (3-mp-mixed) sub-sections and their byte blocks. The "v1.0 idempotent-flip variant ... not expressible in v2" paragraph is gone. The "Same structural shape as enable-mixed" prose is gone. The "the orchestrator chooses (updated) for both directions" prose is gone. Confirmed via `grep -cE "not expressible in v2|enable-mixed|disable-mixed" docs/output-catalog.md` → 0.
