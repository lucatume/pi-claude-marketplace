<objective>
Close the migration/coexistence story (MIRR-06) and prove GC parity for the mirror shape (MIRR-04). Reinstall is network-gated (PRL-07: cached manifests only), so its unpinned arm repairs fs-only from the warm mirror the record points at — it does NOT re-anchor and does NOT materialize a cold mirror (re-anchor happens on install/update, Plan 03). A pre-existing per-sha unpinned record keeps repairing from its per-sha clone until a network-legal update/install re-anchors it. GC needs no code change; this plan verifies the bare-key shape is protected and orphaned per-sha clones sweep.

Purpose: Make old-design unpinned records coexist safely with the new mirror shape, with no persisted migration state, and confirm GC protects mirror-anchored records while sweeping orphans (MIRR-04, MIRR-06).
Output: The reinstall unpinned mirror-repair arm, plus GC and update coexistence tests proving protection + sweep behavior.
</objective>
